EMuLe project first retreat

  1. Home
  2. EMuLe project retreat

On September 17, our EMuLe project team held its very first retreat in Potsdam.

The retreat brought the team together in person to share progress, exchange ideas, and outline plans for the future. Each team member presented the work they had accomplished so far, highlighting both achievements and next steps

A central part of the day was the session led by Dr. Zahra , who presented the overall progress of the project and discussed strategic directions. She also provided constructive feedback to each team member, offering valuable insights on how to refine and strengthen their contributions.

The retreat was not only an opportunity to align on scientific and technical goals, but also to build stronger connections within the team—fostering collaboration, fresh perspectives, and a shared vision for the next phases of EMuLe.
